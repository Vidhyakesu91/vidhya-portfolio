SEO + Content 01
Organic Traffic Turnaround for a B2B Service Brand
Industry: Professional Services · Duration: 3 Months
The Problem
The client had a 5-year-old website with no SEO foundation — zero blog content, thin service pages, and 17 critical technical errors flagged by Google. Organic traffic had plateaued at under 200 monthly visits despite strong offline reputation.
The Solution

Website Audit 02
Full SEO Audit & Redesign Strategy for E-Commerce Store
Industry: Retail E-Commerce · Duration: 6 Weeks
The Problem
High ad spend but poor organic visibility. The store had 400+ pages, 60% of which were duplicate or thin content — cannibalized by their own product variants. Cart abandonment was at 74%, and mobile scores averaged 38/100.
The Solution
Delivered a 47-page audit covering technical health, content gaps, UX issues, and conversion blockers. Built a prioritized roadmap — quick wins for month one, structural fixes for month two, content strategy for month three.

Social Media 03
LinkedIn Authority Campaign for an Independent Consultant
Industry: Coaching & Consulting · Duration: 2 Months
The Problem
An experienced consultant with 12 years of expertise had only 340 LinkedIn followers and zero inbound leads from social. Profile was outdated, posting was inconsistent, and there was no content strategy driving thought leadership.
The Solution
Rebuilt the LinkedIn profile from scratch with keyword-optimized headline and summary. Developed an 8-week content calendar mixing insight posts, carousels, and case-snippets. Executed daily engagement strategy targeting ideal client profiles.
LinkedIn Analytics Canva Buffer Google Sheets
Local SEO 04
Local SEO Domination for a Multi-Location Service Business
Industry: Local Services · Duration: 4 Months
The Problem
A home services company with 3 branches had inconsistent NAP data across 40+ local directories. Google Business Profiles were unoptimized and barely appearing in local map packs — competitors were capturing all the "near me" searches.
The Solution
Standardized NAP across all directories, optimized all 3 GBP listings with categories, Q&A, services, and weekly posts. Built location-specific landing pages with local schema markup and collected 40+ authentic reviews through a structured outreach flow.
Google Business Profile BrightLocal Yoast SEO Search Console
